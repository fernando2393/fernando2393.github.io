About Me

As a Machine Learning Scientist at Expedia Group, my focus is on developing recommendation systems within the lodging and travel environment. I work with big amounts of distributed data, build and evaluate machine learning models, and analyze user behavior to improve the performance of these systems.

With a BSc in Computer Science from the University Carlos III of Madrid and an MSC from KTH Royal Institute of Technology, I have a strong foundation in computing and a deep understanding of machine learning techniques.

In addition to my technical skills, I am fluent in English, Spanish, and French, which allows me to effectively communicate with teams and partners from around the world.

As a lifelong learner, I constantly seek new challenges and opportunities to expand my skills and expertise. I am passionate about technology and innovation and strive to make a meaningful impact on the industry.
